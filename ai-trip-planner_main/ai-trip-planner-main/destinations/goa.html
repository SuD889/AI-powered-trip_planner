About Goa

Goa, India's smallest state, is known for its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese architecture, and water sports. It's a perfect blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures, offering something for every type of traveler.

Things to Do

  • Relax at famous beaches like Baga and Calangute
  • Try water sports at Anjuna Beach
  • Visit historic churches in Old Goa
  • Experience vibrant nightlife
  • Explore spice plantations
  • Visit Fort Aguada

How to Reach

  • By Air: Dabolim Airport (Goa International Airport)
  • By Train: Madgaon and Vasco da Gama railway stations
  • By Road: Connected to major cities via NH66
