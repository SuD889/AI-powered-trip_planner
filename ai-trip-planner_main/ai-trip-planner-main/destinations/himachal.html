Himachal Pradesh - Land of Snow

Location: Northern India
Best Time: March to June (Summer), December to February (Winter)
Average Temperature: -2°C to 28°C
Ideal Duration: 7-10 days

About Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh, nestled in the western Himalayas, is known for its stunning landscapes, ranging from snow-capped mountains to lush valleys. The state offers a perfect blend of adventure, spirituality, and natural beauty.

Popular Destinations

  • Shimla - The colonial summer capital
  • Manali - Adventure sports hub
  • Dharamshala - Home to Dalai Lama
  • Spiti Valley - Cold desert mountain valley
  • Kullu - Valley of Gods

Activities & Experiences

  • Skiing in Kufri
  • Paragliding in Bir Billing
  • Trekking in Great Himalayan National Park
  • River rafting in Kullu
  • Camping in Kasol
