Taj Mahal, Agra

4.9/5 - World Heritage Site
Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh
Best Time: October to March
Entry Fee: ₹1100 (Foreigners), ₹50 (Indians)

About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, India. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

Things to Do

  • Watch the sunrise at the Taj Mahal
  • Take a guided tour to learn about its history
  • Visit the adjacent Agra Fort
  • Explore the Mughal gardens

How to Reach

  • By Air: Agra Airport (25km)
  • By Train: Agra Cantt Railway Station (6km)
  • By Road: Well connected to Delhi and other major cities
