Gokarna - Spiritual Beach Paradise

Location: Karnataka, India
Best Time: October to March
Average Temperature: 24°C to 32°C
Ideal Duration: 3-4 days

About Gokarna

Gokarna, a small temple town on the western coast of India, has transformed into a hidden paradise for beach lovers and spiritual seekers. With its pristine beaches, sacred temples, and laid-back atmosphere, it offers a perfect alternative to the crowded beaches of Goa.

Must-Visit Places

  • Om Beach - Famous beach shaped like Om symbol
  • Mahabaleshwar Temple - Ancient Shiva temple
  • Paradise Beach - Secluded pristine beach
  • Half Moon Beach - Crescent-shaped beach
  • Kudle Beach - Popular for sunset views

Activities & Experiences

  • Beach trekking between beaches
  • Surfing and water sports
  • Temple visits and spiritual experiences
  • Sunset watching at beaches
  • Boat trips to secluded beaches
