Darjeeling

Location: West Bengal, India
Best Time: October to March
Average Temperature: 8°C to 15°C
Ideal Duration: 4-5 days

About Darjeeling

Known as the "Queen of Hills," Darjeeling is famous for its tea plantations, stunning views of the Himalayas, and the UNESCO World Heritage Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. The town offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, colonial architecture, and rich cultural heritage.

Must-Visit Places

  • Tiger Hill - Famous for sunrise views of Kanchenjunga
  • Batasia Loop - Scenic railway loop and war memorial
  • Happy Valley Tea Estate - One of the oldest tea gardens
  • Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park - Home to red pandas
  • Peace Pagoda - Buddhist temple with panoramic views

Local Experiences

  • Take a ride on the Toy Train (DHR)
  • Tea tasting at various estates
  • Visit the Tibetan Refugee Self Help Center
  • Shop at Mall Road
  • Try local Nepali cuisine
