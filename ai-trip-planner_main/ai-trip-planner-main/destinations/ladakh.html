Ladakh - Land of High Passes

Location: Union Territory, India
Best Time: June to September
Average Temperature: -15°C to 30°C
Ideal Duration: 7-10 days

About Ladakh

Ladakh, known as "Little Tibet", is a region of stark beauty and extreme landscapes. With its high-altitude deserts, pristine lakes, and ancient Buddhist monasteries, it offers a unique blend of natural wonder and cultural heritage.

Must-Visit Places

  • Pangong Lake - Famous turquoise lake
  • Nubra Valley - Desert landscape with double-humped camels
  • Leh Palace - Nine-story palace overlooking Leh
  • Khardung La - World's highest motorable pass
  • Thiksey Monastery - Tibetan Buddhist monastery

Activities & Experiences

  • River rafting in Zanskar
  • Mountain biking on high-altitude roads
  • Camping under the stars
  • Monastery visits and meditation
  • Photography at Magnetic Hill
