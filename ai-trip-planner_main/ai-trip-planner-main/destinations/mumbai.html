Mumbai - City of Dreams

Location: Maharashtra, India
Best Time: October to February
Average Temperature: 24°C to 32°C
Ideal Duration: 4-5 days

About Mumbai

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a city of contrasts where tradition meets modernity. From historic colonial architecture to bustling street markets, from Bollywood glamour to Asia's largest slum, Mumbai offers a unique blend of experiences.

Must-Visit Places

  • Gateway of India - Historic arch monument
  • Marine Drive - Famous seaside promenade
  • Colaba Causeway - Popular shopping street
  • Elephanta Caves - UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Juhu Beach - Famous beach with street food

Local Experiences

  • Take a local train ride
  • Try street food at Chowpatty
  • Visit Dharavi - Asia's largest slum
  • Watch sunset at Bandstand
  • Experience Bollywood at Film City
